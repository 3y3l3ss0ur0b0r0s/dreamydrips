Our Mission

Here at Dreamy Drips, we aim to provide diehard potion-lovers and casual consumers alike with a little razzle dazzle in their lives! We pride ourselves in our potions' high-quality ingredients and highly-concentrated magic.

Our History

Established in 200 BC, Dreamy Drips has gone through many changes. One thing that has not changed is the quality of the potions that we craft and the passion that goes into each one.

It all began when a young lady named Camilla Cassia found that her heritage included elven blood. The new and zealous young elf-lady explored many forests in order to get in touch with her heritage. It was in these forests that Camilla Cassia discovered her talents for potion-crafting. One day, she came across an aristocrat who had been robbed and left for dead in the woods. She shared just one potion with him, and from then on, he and many others grew to admire and support Camilla Cassia's potion-crafting. From a passion of a single elf-lady to the collective effort of all elves, humans, and everyone in between who now work in the Dreamy Drips team, we have retained the memory of our roots and strive to spread the natural goodness and magic of our potions to all!
